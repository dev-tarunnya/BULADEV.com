How We Work

Clear Steps. Better Communication. Stronger Results.

Construction and development can feel overwhelming when there is no clear plan. Our process is designed to help clients understand the path from idea to completion. With 20 years of experience, licensed and insured service, certified Lean Six Sigma training, and professional project management, we focus on planning, communication, coordination, and quality review.

Request a Consultation

Initial Consultation

We begin by discussing your project goals, property details, budget expectations, timeline, and overall vision.

Property and Scope Review

We review the project site or property conditions and identify what work may be needed to achieve your goals.

Planning and Proposal

We prepare a project scope, timeline expectations, and cost considerations so you can make informed decisions.

Project Preparation

Before construction begins, we coordinate the details, materials, schedule, team requirements, and project communication expectations.

Construction and Coordination

Our team begins the work according to the approved plan while maintaining communication throughout the project.

Progress Updates

We keep you informed as work moves forward and communicate important updates, adjustments, or decisions.

Quality Review

We review the work at key stages and address details to support a strong final result.

Final Walkthrough and Delivery

Once work is completed, we review the project with you, address final details, and deliver the finished project.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle both residential and commercial construction?

Yes. BULADEV and ASA Construction LLC support both residential and commercial construction projects, including new construction, renovations, improvements, tenant build-outs, and property upgrades.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. We are licensed and insured. This gives clients added confidence that they are working with a professional construction and development team.

How much experience does your team have?

Our team brings 20 years of experience in construction, property improvement, development support, and project coordination.

What does Certified Lean Six Sigma mean for construction clients?

Lean Six Sigma focuses on improving processes, reducing waste, solving problems, and creating more efficient workflows. In construction, this supports better planning, stronger coordination, clearer communication, and improved project control.

Do you provide professional project management?

Yes. Our professional project managers help organize scope, schedule, communication, cost awareness, materials, trade coordination, quality checkpoints, and project closeout.

Can you help with land development?

Yes. BULADEV provides land development support, including project planning, site preparation coordination, property improvement strategy, and construction-readiness support.

Do you work with investors and developers?

Yes. We support real estate investors, developers, and property owners who need construction or development assistance for residential, commercial, or land improvement projects.

How do I start a project?

The first step is to contact us by phone at (313) 444-9734, email at bula@BULADEV.com, or through our website form. We will discuss your project goals, property details, timeline, and next steps.
